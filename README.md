# logbook
A private, offline-first space to write, organize, and think - your way.

## What is Logbook?

Logbook is a simple, privacy-focused alternative to apps like Notion, Obsidian, AppFlowy, Anytype.

Instead of complex systems and technical terms, Logbook uses a more human approach:

- Books = your collections
- Logs = your entries

Everything is designed to feel natural and distraction-free.

## Why Logbook?

Most tools today:

- rely heavily on cloud
- use complex structures
- don't give full control over your data

Logbook focuses on:

- Offline-first - your data stays with you
- Privacy-focused - no forced cloud sync

## Features (current/planned)

- Create and manage Books
- Add Logs inside Books
- Clean writing interface
- Sidebar customization
- Optional cloud sync (user-controlled/pro)

## Philosophy

Logbook is built around the idea that tools should:
- stay out of your way
- respect your privacy
- adapt to your thinking
